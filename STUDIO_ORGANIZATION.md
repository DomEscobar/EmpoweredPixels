## Development Process (AAA Studio Style)

### Sprint Cycle: 2 Weeks

**Week 1:**
- Monday: Sprint planning (CPO prioritizes, teams commit)
- Tue-Fri: Development (Engineering, Art, Design)

**Week 2:**
- Mon-Wed: Feature complete, testing begins
- Thu: Bug fixing, polish
- Friday: Ship or roll decision (CQO decides)

### Decision Matrix

| Decision Type | Decider | Input From |
|--------------|---------|------------|
| Feature Priority | CPO | CEO, Market Research |
| Technical Architecture | CTO | Engineering |
| Release Readiness | CQO | QA, Engineering |
| Monetization | CPO | CEO |
| Major Pivot | CEO | All Leads |

### Communication Rhythm

- **Daily:** Standups (async via heartbeat)
- **Weekly:** Demo day (show shipped features)
- **Bi-weekly:** Retrospective (what worked/didn't)
- **Monthly:** Stakeholder review (Dom & Alex)
